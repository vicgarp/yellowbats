TEARING DOWN THE WALLS

If you're looking for yet another punk rock band (chances are you're not), the Yellowbats are here to solve your problems!
We write songs about leaving town, anxiety, regrets and frustration, everything you've heard before, BUT BETTER!
Since 2019, we're making music inspired by the heroes of punk, power pop and melodic hardcore (you know who they are).
The Yellowbats are Victor Garcia (vocals and guitars), Glauco Forñas (drums) and Gabriel Marques (bass).
